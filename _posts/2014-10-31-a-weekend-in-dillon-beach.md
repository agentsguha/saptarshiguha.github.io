Here some pictures using Porta 400. I developed and scanned them at
Photoworks. For the price they charge, i was hoping for larger resolution images
but no. For nearly all of them, I had to overexpose by 0.3 to 1.3 stops. And
that's how I like it. Some have been taken by a friend of mine. The last 7
pictures are with a Fuji XT1 with the 35mm lens.
More pictures using the Mamiya 645 Pro can be found
[here](https://people.mozilla.com/~sguha/blog/2014/10/30/medium-format-first-time.html)
